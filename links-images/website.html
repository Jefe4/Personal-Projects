Ghost of Tsushima
This game should've won the Game of the Year as it was the fan favorite winning by the landslide even beating TLOU2. With only bring home one award which doesn't truly appearicate how amazing that game is. With a unique and yet satisfiating combat system that rewarded you for exploring the world to find materials to upgrade Jin the main character. The scenery is beautiful and rich in color in details with the environment which led them to winning Best Art Direction award. Also winning best game award in Japan which is the first American made game to win it there. The game was about when the Mongol Empire invaded Tsushima in 1274. The showed the Japanese culture and customs they do in the game as the characters would often bow to show respect and variety of other customs. This brought awareness to the gamers of Japanese culture and insight of the invasion that happened. The game recieved a 9/10 from IGN as it's a game worth playing.
